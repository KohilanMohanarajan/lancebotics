Team 200C

Lancebotics Team 200C was formed from Leaside’s very own TEJ4 class, consisting of 16 dedicated, talented and innovative students. Each of them have their individual strengths that contribute to the class team as a whole. After two years of working together, they have gained experience and developed trust in one another, and for their third year, the team will participate in the VEX Robotics Competitions as a part of the course curriculum. Although their primary goal is to qualify for Worlds, they ultimately aspire to win the entire competition. In terms of progress, Team C has completed their designs for the robot and the robot’s base. They are currently working on the programming of the robot as well as the loading and shooting systems. All in all, the team has the potential to fulfill their goals, and excel in the 2015-2016 VEX Robotics Competitions.
